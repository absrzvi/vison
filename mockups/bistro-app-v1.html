📊 Demand indicator
High/Medium/Low pill in header gives instant read. Footfall sparkline shows whether demand is rising or falling — not just current state.
🧍 Live queue count
Camera detection of people queuing at the bistro counter. Visual person icons make it immediately scannable without reading numbers.
🚆 Coach load direction
Arrows show which coaches are likely to send passengers toward the bistro car. High-load coaches on either side = incoming rush. Bistro car marked with star.
🚉 Boarding predictions
APC + reservation data predicts how many passengers board at each upcoming stop. Bistro staff can prep before arrival, not after.
📦 Stock alert
Cross-references current stock levels (VLAN 46 bistro telemetry) with predicted demand. Tells staff what to restock at next depot stop before they run out.
