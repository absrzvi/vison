Hailo-8 insights are interpretations, not raw numbers. The Hailo-8 gives Claudia three things the current system cannot: per-zone congestion (vestibule vs. seating area vs. luggage rack), confidence scores on every read, and pattern analysis across the 7-day history. The "→ Suggest action to Conrad" link is the closed-loop feature — Claudia can push a suggested action to the conductor's app without a phone call.
