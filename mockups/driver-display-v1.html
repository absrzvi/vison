🚪 Doors — always visible
Per-door clear/blocked status is the primary element. Always shown, always the largest element. Blocked door pulses red. Driver must not depart with a blocked door.
⚠️ Faults — critical only
Only faults above a configurable severity threshold appear here. HVAC watch states, minor warnings — not shown. Only show what requires driver awareness. Non-critical faults go to Conductor/Technician apps only.
🚉 Platform flag — advisory only
Shown when AI predicts heavy boarding at next stop. Advisory — driver decides whether to allow extra dwell. Not an alarm, lower visual weight than door status.
Zero interaction
No buttons, no taps, no navigation. Display only. Driver interacts with their existing Stadler cab systems for all control functions. This display is information only.
