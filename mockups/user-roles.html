Who are the users of this platform?

Two Hailo-8 modules, two services — let's map who uses what before designing any screens

🚂 ONBOARD — On the train
🧑‍✈️ Conductor / Train Manager
Handheld device. Needs passenger load per coach, safety alerts, unattended luggage, door issues. Also wants diagnostic alerts — "what's wrong on my train right now?"
🍽️ Bistro / Café Staff
Handheld or fixed tablet. Wants to see how many people are heading their way, queue length, coach congestion to anticipate demand.
🚗 Driver
Cab display. Needs door obstruction alerts before departure, critical safety alerts, and key diagnostic faults only. Minimal UI — must not distract.
🔧 Onboard Technician
Handheld. Full diagnostics view — Stadler alarm history, fault patterns, natural language query ("why is the HVAC alarm firing?"), maintenance triggers.
🏢 LANDSIDE — Off the train
🎛️ OEBB Control Centre Operator
Web dashboard. Fleet-wide occupancy heatmap, congestion alerts, safety incidents across all trains, real-time and historical views.
🔩 Fleet Maintenance Manager
Web dashboard. Predictive fault trends, maintenance work order triggers, alarm pattern history per train, cleaning schedules from occupancy data.
📊 OEBB Capacity Planner
Analytics reports. Ridership patterns, dwell times by stop, peak loads by route and time of day. Monthly data subscription product.
🚉 Platform Staff / Station Manager
Simple display or tablet. Incoming train occupancy per coach, accessibility needs flagged, predictive overcrowding so they can guide passengers on the platform.
🧍 Passengers also see the Smart Travel portal page (coach occupancy display) on Wi-Fi — but they're consumers of the output, not users of an interface we design.
