# Business Goals — OEBB Smart Rail Passenger Intelligence

**Phase:** 2 — Trigger Map
**Date:** 2026-05-14

---

## Goal 1: Become the Infrastructure-Anchored Intelligence Provider for European Rail

The primary outcome: Nomad Digital transforms from a connectivity vendor into a managed AI service company — one whose value compounds as data accumulates, models improve, and operators expand. This is the business Nomad Digital is building, not just the product it is shipping.

### SMART Objectives
- **1.1** Renew or expand ÖBB pilot contract within 3 months of go-live, converting to multi-year SaaS agreement
- **1.2** Sign at least one additional European rail operator onto the Passenger Intelligence platform within 12 months of ÖBB go-live
- **1.3** Achieve per-train monthly subscription revenue that exceeds hardware + operational cost by ≥40% gross margin

---

## Goal 2: Make ÖBB Staff Measurably More Effective

The prerequisite: the service must create observable, defensible operational improvement for ÖBB. Without this, renewal fails and the blueprint collapses. Staff effectiveness is not a soft outcome — it needs to be provable in numbers ÖBB can put in a business case.

### SMART Objectives
- **2.1** Reduce conductor time spent on manual occupancy assessment by ≥50% per journey (measured via time-motion study on pilot trains)
- **2.2** Reduce delayed departures attributable to door obstruction incidents by ≥30% on pilot fleet within pilot period
- **2.3** Achieve ≥80% adoption rate among pilot-fleet conductors within 4 weeks of launch (measured by daily active use)

---

## Goal 3: Prove Privacy-by-Design at Scale

The second prerequisite: Nomad Digital must demonstrate that AI-powered passenger intelligence can be delivered fully compliant with European data protection law — no raw video stored, no biometric data, no regulatory exposure. This is both a legal necessity and a sales differentiator for privacy-conscious operators.

### SMART Objectives
- **3.1** Zero raw video transmitted off-vehicle across entire pilot period (verified by technical audit)
- **3.2** Pass ÖBB data protection review before pilot go-live
- **3.3** Publish replicable privacy architecture documentation that can be used in future operator procurement responses
