## 3. Conflicts to resolve (architect decision required)

### ‚öÝÔ∏è Conflict A ‚Äî Detector license: ADR-16 specifies `yolov8m.hef` (AGPL), violating the permissive-only constraint
- **ADR-16** names **`yolov8m.hef`** as the counting detector and **`yolov8m_pose.hef`** for P1 pose.
- YOLOv8 (Ultralytics) is **AGPL-3.0 ‚Äî including the model weights**, not just the repo (confirmed in the 2026-06-01 Hailo repo research, 3-0 verified against ultralytics.com/license).
- This is **incompatible with the commercial Insights-as-a-Service product** without a paid Ultralytics Enterprise License.
- **Options:**
  1. **Swap to YOLOX** (Apache-2.0) ‚Äî already in the Hailo Model Zoo as Hailo-8 HEFs (`yolox_s_leaky` etc.); pairs with ByteTrack/`hailotracker`. Recommended.
  2. Purchase Ultralytics Enterprise License (recurring cost, per-product).
  3. Accept YOLOv8 for the PoC only, with a documented swap-before-commercial gate.
- **Pose (`yolov8m_pose`)** has the same AGPL issue ‚Äî needs a permissive pose model if pose stays in scope (ADR-16 restricts pose to accessibility/fall-detection P1 cameras only).

### ‚öÝÔ∏è Conflict B ‚Äî Door-open gating is a new optimisation not in any ADR
- Proposed this session: run each door camera only while its door is open (gated on **VLAN 7 Stadler SNMP** / **VLAN 2 ZFR** door-open signal), so peak concurrent load is 6‚Äì12 streams, not 24.
- **Not reflected in ADR-15.** ADR-15 implies continuous tripwire monitoring.
- **Benefit:** large TOPS headroom, lower power, counting aligned to dwell window.
- **Cost:** adds a dependency on the door-open signal being wired into `inference`; tripwire must handle camera activate/deactivate transitions cleanly (cold-start seeding per ADR-17 ¬ß6 becomes relevant per door-open cycle).
- **Decision needed:** is gating in PoC scope, or a Phase 2 optimisation? If the 24 streams already fit the 25‚Äì30 budget at the target fps, gating may be deferrable.

## 4. Open item ‚Äî door‚Üícamera physical mapping (blocks `cameras.json`)

The IP sheet gives every camera's **network identity** but **not its mount position**. ADR-15 needs each door-threshold camera identified in `cameras.json` with a tripwire polygon. To produce the **door ‚Üí camera ID ‚Üí IP** table, one of these is required:
- `Verkabelungsschema` (cabling drawing, 30.05.2024 ‚Äî the basis of this IP sheet), or
- Saloon **GA layout** drawing for the DOSTO 6-car.

Look for: camera positions relative to door/vestibule markings (GA), or cable-run labels tying a camera ID to a door zone (Verkabelungsschema).

**Until that drawing is available, the door-camera subset of the 53 cannot be pinned, and `cameras.json` tripwire configs cannot be authored.**
